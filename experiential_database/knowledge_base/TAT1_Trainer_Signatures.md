# TAT1 Knowledge Base: Trainer Signatures

**Source:** RC Research Report, ISSUE-015 (2026-03-22)

---

## Core Principle

Certain trainers exhibit statistically significant, repeatable patterns of success that the market may under-price. Identifying and modelling these "signatures" is a key source of proprietary edge.
