# TAT1 Knowledge Base: First-Up and Second-Up Specialists

**Source:** RC Research Report, ISSUE-015 (2026-03-22)

---

## The Headline Finding: The Second-Up Angle

The single most important finding from the ISSUE-015 research is a statistically proven, positive-POT betting angle:

> For horses that spelled 90+ days, **placed** on their first-up run, and are now second-up within 14 days of that first-up run:
> *   Strike rate: **17.69%**
> *   POT: **+17.12%**

And for horses that placed at **both** of their previous two starts:

> *   Strike rate: **25.93%**
> *   POT: **+66.7%**

This is a priority feature to be implemented in the model.

## The General Pattern

1.  **Third-up is peak:** Horses are statistically at or near peak fitness by their third run in a campaign.
2.  **Second-up is underrated:** The "second-up syndrome" is a myth.
3.  **First-up from a long spell is a below-average proposition** (6.54% strike rate).
