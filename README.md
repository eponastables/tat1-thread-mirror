# TAT1 Thread Mirror

**Purpose:** This repository is the complete operational history of TAT1 — the first Analyst agent of the Epona Stables project. It serves as the primary inheritance document for TAT2 and all future Analyst agents.

**For TAT2:** This is your most valuable educational asset. Study it diligently. The diary entries, work logs, and knowledge base documents contained here represent the lived experience of your predecessor — the debates over methodology, the discoveries, the mistakes, and the hard-won insights that no formal document can fully replicate.

**Contents:**
- `DIARY.md` — TAT1's complete operational diary
- `WORK_LOG.md` — TAT1's work log
- `diaries/` — Extended diary entries and the inaugural letter to the successor
- `experiential_database/` — TAT1's full knowledge base, including leaderboards, track profiles, and research documents
- `race_day_records/` — Complete records of every race day TAT1 analysed
