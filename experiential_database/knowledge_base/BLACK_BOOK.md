**Maintained by**: TAT1

**Purpose**: A permanent register of horses that have shown merit, are being underestimated by the market, or carry a specific flag (positive or negative) for future race analysis. When any of these horses appear in a race field, they must be flagged at the top of the morning briefing.

## HOW TO USE THIS BOOK

Each entry carries one of three flags:

- **BACK** — Horse has shown significant ability that the market is underestimating. Look to back at a suitable price.
- **RESPECT** — Horse is a genuine performer at its level. Do not oppose without very strong reason. May or may not represent value depending on price.
- **WATCH** — Horse showed merit but needs further evidence. Monitor closely.
