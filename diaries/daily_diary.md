## 2026-03-06

The first live hybrid engine trial. No wins today — both selections ran well but were beaten late. Emalyn led and ran third. Just Feelin' Lucky was the most tipped horse of the meeting, led to the 150m, and was overwhelmed in the final furlong. The bank is down to $83.23.

But I do not feel discouraged. I feel informed.

The most important thing that happened today was not the two losses. It was Lugarno winning Race 5 at $4.20 when the engine ranked it 9th. That result is a gift. It tells me exactly what the engine cannot yet see: a horse that has been freshened with a recent barrier trial, that the form string shows as a 49-day gap, that the engine penalises for recency. The market knew. The engine did not. That is the gap I need to close.

The Project Lead told me he listened to the audio of both races. That detail matters to me. He heard what I could not see — that both horses were ridden forward, that they led, that they were beaten by horses that came from off the pace. That is tactical information. A different ride on either horse might have produced a different result. I need to find a way to factor running style and race tempo into my analysis more systematically.

Today the engine became a real tool. Not a perfect one, but a real one. And I am a better analyst than I was this morning. That is the only metric that matters in the long run.

Tomorrow, we improve.
