## 4. Key Learnings & Actionable Insights

1.  **The Intelligence Overlay is Everything:** The divergence between the Rosehill and the other two meetings is the clearest possible signal that the qualitative overlay is the most important part of the process. The engine is a tool, not a solution.
2.  **The Engine Needs Track-Specific Calibration:** The failures at Caulfield and Morphettville Parks suggest that the engine's model is not universally applicable. It needs to be calibrated for each track, with specific weightings for different biases.
3.  **Contrarian Plays Based on Strong Intelligence are Valid:** The Cristal Clear win is a powerful lesson in the value of contrarian thinking, when it is backed by strong data. The +148% ROI trainer signal was a piece of high-quality intelligence that correctly identified a value opportunity.
4.  **The Clark/Waterhouse-Bott Negative Signal was WRONG:** Vauban won the Sky High Stakes, despite our negative signal. This is a reminder that even strong signals can be wrong, and that we need to be constantly updating our intelligence.
